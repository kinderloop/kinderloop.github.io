Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

When you sign up for the first time you will automatically have unlimited access for 30-days to your own private Kinderloop. You can add as many children as you need, document and invite family members that you would like to share the trial with. At the end of the free trial you can seemlessly upgrade to a paying account which will unlock additional features.

Long-term commitments?

We do not charge a cancellation fee but ask that you give us one months notice, we will not make you sign any contracts.

Who owns the images/content I add?

Any posts added to YOUR Kinderloop are owned by you.

Its your content, not ours and not the families - it cannot be shared anywhere unless you give express approval.

What if I want to remove children?

You can archive or remove children at any time from your Kinderloop. Don't worry, their posts won't be deleted, and family will still be able to access their profiles.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes. If you are responsible for a centre with more than 30 children, or a group of early learning services get in touch with us to chat about group discounts.

Does it cost parents and families to use?

No, there isn't a charge for parents or family members, they can download and use the free app once invited by their child care educator.
