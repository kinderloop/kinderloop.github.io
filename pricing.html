Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

When you sign up for the first time you will automatically have unlimited access for 30-days to Kinderloop. You can add as many children as you need, post and invite anyone you would like to share the trial with. At the end of the free trial you will need to upgrade to a paying account to continue using Kinderloop.

Long-term commitments or hidden fees?

No. You can upgrade or cancel at anytime. We do not charge a cancellation fee and we will not make you sign any contracts.

Who owns the posts I add?

Any posts added to YOUR Kinderloop are owned by the administrator of the child the story was added for.

They're your pictures, not ours - we don't own them, we don't sell them, we don't share them.

What if I want to remove children?

You can remove children at any time from your Kinderloop. Don't worry, their posts won't be deleted, and family will still be able to access their profiles. You can then add more child profiles to replace those you removed.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes. If you are responsible for a centre with more than 80 children, or a group of early learning services get in touch with us for more information.

Does it cost parents and families to use?

No, there isn't a charge for parents or family members, they can download and use the app for free once invited by their child care provider.
